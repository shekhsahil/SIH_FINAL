WELCOME TO SOIL SENSE






INSTRUCTIONS

  • New users will have to add their crop field system.
  • For this, the individual will have to click on the ‘Add a New System’ button available on the ‘Home’ page.
  • On clicking the button, he will be redirected to a page where he will have to add the configuration of his field namely, Crop Type, Crop Name, Crop Stage, and Crop Area.
  • Clicking the ‘SAVE’ button on the ‘Configuration page’ , the system will save the details and the user will be redirected to the ‘Dashboard’ page of the system.
  • In the ‘Dashboard’ page, first the user will have to grant his location permission.
  • Then, user can easily visualize the soil moisture data from the AI-based algorithm and also the real-time soil moisture data from the sensors. The Dashboard also shows the weather details of the user’s location.
  • To add another system, the user will have to navigate back to the ‘Home’ page and again click on the ‘Add a New Monitoring System’ button.
  • The already added system can be found in the ‘Home’ page of the application above the ‘Add a New Monitoring System’  button.
